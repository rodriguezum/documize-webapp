Common Questions

Can you accept a Purchase Order or a government purchase request?

Yes. Please email your paperwork to sales@documize.com and we'l do the rest.

Can you demo Documize to my team?

Of course, contact contact the sales team and we’ll happily schedule a product for your team.

Do you provide migration assistance?

We can and do assist customers with data migration from SharePoint and Confluence.

Is this a one-time fee?

The licensing model is an annual product subscription providing access to the latest release and technical support.

What are the server requirements?

Available on Windows, Linux or MacOS servers. MySQL (v5.7.10+) or Percona Server (v5.7.16-10+) is required.

Where are you based?

We are headquartered out of San Francisco and have a presence in London, UK.

Do you provide competitive product discounts?

Shhh. Get in touch.

Can we cancel?

Yes you can cancel your subscription at any time with no additional cost.

Does your software 'call home' or otherwise track in-app activity?

We collect telemetry and app usage information, for non-evil purposes, which you can disable from your admin console.

Where can I find and review your Privacy, Terms and Security policy?

Please see the link in the footer that is located at the bottom of every page.

What is the latest release?

v1.55.0 is the latest Documize release.

What are the deployment requirements?

Documize ships as a single binary that is the HTTP/2 web server, front-end and back-end. It's about a 20MB file that has ZERO runtime dependencies. No Java, PHP, .NET of Ruby runtime needed.

Can I talk to someone?

Of course, just get in touch and schedule a phone call.
